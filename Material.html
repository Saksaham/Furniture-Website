Advantages of Teak Wood

  • Teak Wood has a dense coarse structure and is heavy which gives it strength and durabilit

  • Obtained from broad-leaved deciduous trees, Teak Wood is really strong.

  • Hence Teak Wood Furniture is known for its strength and durability.

  • This weather resistivity makes Teak Wood a perfect material for Outdoor Furniture.

  • Teak Wood is Carving Friendly Due to its dense structure.
